NAME
    freetriva - an online command-line trivia game 

SYNOPSIS
    freetriva [OPTION]...

DESCRIPTION
    Generates a command-line trivia through communication with a public API.
    Don't include the options if you want default values or any

    --questions=SIZE
        default size is 10 and maximum is 50

    --category=ID
        ID should be the below 
            General Knowledge: 1
            Books: 2
            Film: 3
            Music: 4
            Musicals & Theatres: 5
            Television: 6
            Video Games: 7
            Board Games: 8
            Nature: 9
            Computers: 10
            Mathematics: 11
            Mythology: 12
            Sports: 13
            Geography: 14
            History: 15
            Politics: 16
            Art: 17
            Celebrities: 18
            Animals: 19
            Vehicles: 20
            Comics: 21
            Gadgets: 22
            Japanese Anime & Manga: 23
            Cartoon & Animations: 24

    --difficulty=LEVEL
        LEVEL should be the below
            easy
            medium
            hard

    --type=NUM  
        NUM = 0
            multiple-choice
        NUM = 1
            true-false

;
